Outrun Life

Welcome to the future... of our past...

When you don't know where to run, when you don't know where to hide, your best bet is to get in you car, insert your favorite cassette, and ride into the horizon with nothing but the music as your company. The neon lights will guide your way...

A new city, a new beginning, and new enemies... You know they'll find you here, they are everywhere, but the show must go on. You know your hacking skills will get you to places, and they will be your undoing too. But this is the life you chose, and you must live with the consecuences.
